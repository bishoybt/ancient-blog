My love story with computers

     Now you got all about me. If you wanna know me more, you are invited to know my story with computers . . .

I, as you can see; like computers & net design. It began with me since I was 7, when I had a small BASIC only computer. I played with it most, but from time to time I used to write programs. The end was I knew all code structure, heard about machine language, copied one machine program, no idea on how it works.
After that, I got a 386 then 486DX and walked a long road in BASIC then PASCAL then C, and at last I found my interest in machine language & Assembly. But I was a little disappointed because what I have to build in years by assembly, is built in seconds by other high languages.
Well, now I'm learning HTML and JavaScript, and am starting to be "visualized".

I have got a lot of interests, and may be more important to me than computers. But I've found that this is the only interest I love to share with my site visitors.
